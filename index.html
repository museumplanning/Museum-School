About the Program

More than a
course.
A career pathway.

Museum School is a professional training program built around the same frameworks used by leading museums across North America. Created by Mark Walhimer — author of Museums 101 and Designing Museum Experiences — it goes far beyond passive video courses.

You'll complete real project briefs, receive detailed feedback, and build a portfolio of museum planning work. Students who excel gain access to a curated talent pool, where Museum Planning LLC draws from for paid, real-world consulting projects.

This is training with skin in the game — for both of us.

Curriculum

Eight weeks.
Six disciplines.

01

Museum Fundamentals

History, governance, mission development, and the landscape of museum typologies — art, science, history, children's, and beyond.

02

Exhibition Design

Flow, narrative, interactivity, and visitor experience. You'll develop a full concept brief for an original exhibition.

03

Collections Management

Acquisition, cataloging, conservation ethics, and digital collections. Grounded in AAM and ICOM standards.

04

Education & Programming

Designing public programs, school partnerships, and community engagement that connect audiences to collections.

05

Fundraising & Finance

Grant writing, earned revenue, capital campaigns, and building sustainable museum business models.

06

Museum Planning

Feasibility studies, master planning, and the consulting deliverables that clients actually pay for. Real-world applications.
